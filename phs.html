Stratumc

IRI http://linked.data.gov.au/def/phs#Stratum
Description

Thing or material on which a pest is searched for or found. A superclass for host or substrate. Stratum is a 'terminological', not an 'instance' class; observation records ('instances') should use either 'Host' or 'Substrate'.
Sub-classes Substratec
