Description

This is a Profile of Darwin Core, GeoSPARQL and several other ontlogies/models related to species observation. It is intended for use with plant health surveillance conducted by Australian biosecurity agencies and industry to maintain food quality, prevent the spread of pests and facilitate trade in foodstuffs.
